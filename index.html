Auden Thomas Anderson

  • Matrix

    Matrix is an experimental fusion excerpt I constructed to match the scene of a character walking through tough terrain for countless years - this led me to call it matrix as a metaphor to resemble this character's journey. My inspiration was taken from an artistic short film I experienced by Kehinde Wiley called in search of the miraculous. The trap beat drum pattern (i.e. running hi hats/open hats and claps on beats 2 and 4) gives the track a driving pulse to represent the movement of my character.

  • D Minor Concerto (unfinished)

    D Minor Concerto: This is an unfinished Baroque Concerto in D minor, that I created as a music A Level composition. I used the Logic stocks instruments to create this concerto. There are three solo instruments in this piece: the harpsichord, flute and oboe - whilst the strings are used as an accompaniment. To improve the work, my intention is to use the plug-in Omnisphere as my VST's, and further to complete this piece of music, I will add a final Episode towards the end of the extract and then conclude the piece with the last ritornello in the Tonic Key.

  • Jungle Space

    Jungle Space was an early house piece I created using Logic stock loops. This was a piece I made to challenge myself in terms of constructing an afro-house styled track that continuously engaged the listener, whilst using your traditional repeated house music format - this includes two main synth melodies; four on the floor kick drum; shakers, tambourines, congas etc. to make up the percussion. The use of the marimba and conga drums was to establish links of a fusion between afro beats and house music.
